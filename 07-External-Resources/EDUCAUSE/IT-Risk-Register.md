## 3. Strategic Risk Areas (EDUCAUSE Model)
* **Reputational:** Risks that hurt the institution's image.
* **Strategic:** Risks that block the mission/goals.
* **Financial:** Risks causing monetary loss.
* **Operational:** Day-to-day system failures.
